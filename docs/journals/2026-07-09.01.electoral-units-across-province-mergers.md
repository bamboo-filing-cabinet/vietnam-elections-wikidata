# Electoral units across the 2025 province mergers

Written 2026-07-09. **Extends** `2026-07-06.03` (persistent-unit model) with
guidance for the XV → XVI transition where provinces were merged.

## The problem

The `2026-07-06.03` decision says electoral units are persistent items that
span elections with stable composition, and the 2025 administrative reform
"begins a fresh generation of unit items." But what exactly does "fresh
generation" mean when:

1. The **territory** of a unit is unchanged (same communes/wards), but
2. The **province** it belongs to changed (merged), and
3. Its **number** changed, and
4. Its **seat count** changed?

Case study: Lào Cai / Yên Bái.

## The facts

### XV (2021) — two separate provinces, 4 units total

| Province | Unit | Components | Seats |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Yên Bái | 1 | TP Yên Bái, Yên Bình, Trấn Yên, Lục Yên | 3 |
| Yên Bái | 2 | TX Nghĩa Lộ, Văn Chấn, Trạm Tấu, Mù Cang Chải, Văn Yên | 3 |
| Lào Cai | 1 | TP Lào Cai, Bát Xát, Bắc Hà, Mường Khương, Si Ma Cai | 3 |
| Lào Cai | 2 | TX Sa Pa, Bảo Thắng, Bảo Yên, Văn Bàn | 3 |

Total: 12 seats across 2 provinces.

### XVI (2026) — merged province "Tỉnh Lào Cai", 4 units

| Unit | Components (now communes/wards) | Seats | Successor of |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Yên Bái area (Âu Lâu, Yên Bình, Lục Yên, Trấn Yên...) | 3 | Yên Bái unit 1 |
| 2 | Nghĩa Lộ area (Trạm Tấu, Mù Cang Chải, Văn Chấn...) | 2 | Yên Bái unit 2 |
| 3 | Lào Cai city area (Bát Xát, Mường Khương, Bắc Hà...) | 3 | Lào Cai unit 1 |
| 4 | Sa Pa area (Bảo Thắng, Bảo Yên, Văn Bàn) | 2 | Lào Cai unit 2 |

Total: 10 seats across 1 merged province.

### Wikidata state

- Yên Bái province (Q36349): P31 = "former provinces of Vietnam",
  P576 = 12 June 2025, P7888 (merged into) = Lào Cai (Q36446).
- Lào Cai province (Q36446): same QID pre- and post-merge, P2046 area
  jumped from 6,364 km² to 13,257 km² in June 2025.
- The old districts (Sa Pa, Bảo Thắng, etc.) were abolished as
  *huyện*/*thị xã* and replaced by communes/wards — but their QIDs
  still exist as former entities.

## Analysis: is XV Lào Cai unit 2 the "same" as XVI Lào Cai unit 4?

Arguments for **same entity** (extend the persistent item):
- The territory is functionally identical — the same geographic area,
  the same population, the same polling stations.
- The components (Sa Pa, Bảo Thắng, Bảo Yên, Văn Bàn) are the same
  set of areas, just now listed at commune level instead of district level
  because the district tier was abolished.
- The unit still exists; it was not dissolved. It was re-determined under
  a new resolution, as happens every election (Law 85/2015 Art. 10).

Arguments for **different entity** (new item):
- The unit number changed (2 → 4).
- The seat count changed (3 → 2).
- The containing province changed identity — it's now "Lào Cai" that
  absorbed Yên Bái, not the old stand-alone Lào Cai.
- The numbering namespace changed: "unit 2 of Lào Cai" in XV was one of
  2 units; "unit 4 of Lào Cai" in XVI is one of 4 units in a province
  that covers twice the area.

## Decision: same entity, extended

The unit **persists**. The core identity of an electoral unit is its
geographic territory — which voters it serves. That didn't change. What
changed is metadata *about* the unit:

- **Number**: a label, not an identity. The number is an artifact of how
  the National Election Council sequences units within a province. When
  Yên Bái's units got folded into Lào Cai's numbering, the old Lào Cai
  units were pushed from 1–2 to 3–4. The unit didn't move; its neighbors
  did.
- **Seat count**: varies between elections based on population. A unit
  losing a seat doesn't become a different unit, just like a US
  congressional district losing a seat in reapportionment doesn't become
  a new district.
- **Province**: the province is the administrative container, not part of
  the unit's core identity. The unit covers the Sa Pa / Bảo Thắng / Bảo
  Yên / Văn Bàn area regardless of whether that area is called "Lào Cai
  province" or "part of merged Lào Cai province."

This is analogous to how Wikidata handles US congressional districts
after redistricting: the entity persists if the territory is materially
the same, even if the number or state context changes.

### What actually changed: the label and qualifiers, not the identity

On the persistent item:
- The **label** can note the name change (or use a generic form).
- **P131** (located in) gets a new qualified value for the XVI era
  pointing at Q36446 (which is now the merged province).
- **P1342** (number of seats) gets per-term qualified values: 3 for XV,
  2 for XVI.
- **P527** (has part) components get additional P2937 qualifiers for the
  16th NA term.
- The description is updated to reflect the full span.

### Labeling

The unit was "Đơn vị bầu cử số 2 tỉnh Lào Cai" in XV and is now
"Đơn vị bầu cử số 4 tỉnh Lào Cai" in XVI. The number is not stable.
Options:

1. **Use the current number** and add aliases for old numbers.
   - en: `Lào Cai electoral unit No. 4` (alias: `Lào Cai electoral unit No. 2`)
   - Problem: confusing when reading XV-era statements.

2. **Use a compound label** like `Lào Cai electoral unit No. 2/4`.
   - Non-standard, ugly.

3. **Use a geographic label** that avoids the number entirely.
   - en: `Sa Pa – Bảo Thắng – Bảo Yên – Văn Bàn electoral unit`
   - Problem: Wikidata convention for Vietnamese units uses numbers.

4. **Label by era of creation, add alias.**
   - en: `Lào Cai electoral unit No. 2` (the number when the item was
     first created/verified). Alias: `Lào Cai electoral unit No. 4`.
   - The description disambiguates: "...numbered No. 2 (15th term) and
     No. 4 (16th term)".

**Decision: option 4.** Use the number from the earliest verified term as
the canonical label, add later numbers as aliases, and explain in the
description. This is stable (the label won't change each election) and
matches how historical districts are typically labeled.

### The mirror case: Yên Bái units → Lào Cai units 1–2

The same logic applies. Yên Bái unit 1 persists as "Lào Cai unit 1" in
XVI — same territory (Yên Bái city area), just renumbered and
re-provinced.

- en label: `Yên Bái electoral unit No. 1`
- alias: `Lào Cai electoral unit No. 1`
- P131 = Yên Bái (Q36349) for XV, Lào Cai (Q36446) for XVI

These items' P131 now points to a province that **no longer exists**
(Q36349, "former province") for the XV era and a different province
(Q36446) for the XVI era. That's fine — P131 qualified by P2937 handles
this cleanly.

## Revised item pattern (extending 2026-07-06.03)

For units that persist across the 2025 reform:

**P131 (located in)** — qualified per term:
- P131 = old province QID, qualified P2937 = NA terms pre-reform
- P131 = new/merged province QID, qualified P2937 = NA terms post-reform

**P1342 (number of seats)** — qualified per term if it changed:
- P1342 = 3, qualified P2937 = Q107979136 (15th)
- P1342 = 2, qualified P2937 = Q138968785 (16th)

**P527 (has part)** — components may need re-qualification:
- Old district QIDs may have P576 (dissolved). The P527 claim stays with
  P2937 qualifiers for the terms the district existed.
- Post-reform commune QIDs may be added with P2937 for XVI if we track
  at that granularity (optional — the pre-reform district QIDs still
  denote the same territory even if the admin entity was abolished).

**Description** — state the full span and note the renumbering, e.g.:
"electoral unit for National Assembly of Vietnam elections (15th–16th
terms, 2021–2026); comprised Sa Pa, Bảo Thắng, Bảo Yên and Văn Bàn;
numbered No. 2 (15th term, Lào Cai province) and No. 4 (16th term,
merged Lào Cai province); 3 seats (15th) / 2 seats (16th)"

## Scope and caveats

- This analysis covers the **Lào Cai / Yên Bái** merger. The same
  pattern should apply to other merged provinces (e.g. Hà Giang), but
  each must be verified — some mergers may have actually reshuffled
  territory between units.
- "Same territory" is verified at the district level. At the commune
  level, the 2025 reform also merged communes, so the *number* of
  sub-units changed even if the aggregate territory is the same. We
  consider this equivalent (no voter moved between electoral units).
- If a future election reshuffles the territory (moves communes between
  units), that would be a composition change warranting a new item per
  `2026-07-06.03`.

## Impact on issue #3 (Sùng A Lềnh)

The issue currently calls for creating two separate electoral unit items
(XV unit 2 and XVI unit 4). Per this decision, only **one item** is
needed:

- en label: `Lào Cai electoral unit No. 2`
- alias: `Lào Cai electoral unit No. 4`
- P131 = Q36446 (Lào Cai), qualified P2937 for both XV and XVI
- P1342 = 3 (XV), 2 (XVI)
- P527 = Sa Pa, Bảo Thắng, Bảo Yên, Văn Bàn — each qualified P2937
  for both terms

Both of Sùng A Lềnh's P39 statements (XV and XVI) point P768 at the
**same** unit item.
