# Modeling Vietnamese NA electoral units on Wikidata

Decided 2026-07-06. **Supersedes** two earlier positions:
- `2026-07-04.03` ("do not create constituency items") — reversed; we do create them.
- The initial per-cycle draft in this note's history — **reversed**: units are
  **persistent**, not per-election. See evidence below.

## Decision: one persistent item per electoral unit

An electoral unit (đơn vị bầu cử) is modeled as a **single Wikidata item** that
spans the elections in which it existed with a stable composition, and is ended
(P576) when a major reorganization supersedes it. This mirrors how US
congressional districts are modeled: a continuing entity, periodically
re-established, occasionally reorganized.

`P768` (electoral district) on each delegate's P39 statement points at this
persistent unit item.

## Evidence: units carry real continuity (primary sources)

Thái Nguyên province, all three units, 2016 (NA14) vs 2021 (NA15):

| Unit | 2016 — Resolution 53/NQ-HĐBCQG | 2021 — our data |
| --- | --- | --- |
| No. 1 | Đại Từ, Định Hóa, Phú Lương — 2 seats | same — 2 seats |
| No. 2 | TP Thái Nguyên, Đồng Hỷ, Võ Nhai — 3 seats | same — 3 seats |
| No. 3 | Sông Công, Phổ Yên, Phú Bình — 2 seats | same — 2 seats |

**Identical** across both elections — composition, numbering, and seat counts.
So units are not reshuffled each election; they persist.

The 2021 → 2026 change (Đồng Hỷ/Võ Nhai regrouped into unit No. 1, components
now wards/communes) was caused by the **2025 administrative reform** (province
merger + abolition of the district tier) — a once-in-generations discontinuity,
not routine redrawing.

Law No. 85/2015/QH13 Art. 10: units are recalculated from population each
election and published 80 days before election day — so they are legally
re-determined, but when the underlying geography is stable the output is
identical (as 2016↔2021 shows). "Identical re-determination" is strong grounds
to treat it as one continuing entity.

**Caveats:** only Thái Nguyên verified, only 2016↔2021; continuity is empirical
(identical output), not a legal statement of same-entity; how far back it runs
(2011?) is unchecked.

## Two layers — keep distinct

- **Component areas** (districts, wards, communes, provinces): standing
  administrative entities → **one item each**, with `dissolved/abolished (P576)`
  when abolished (e.g. Đồng Hỷ *huyện*, ended by the 2025 reform). Already on
  Wikidata; linked via P527.
- **Electoral unit** (đơn vị bầu cử No. N): the persistent grouping *of* those
  areas → **one item**, ended (P576) at the 2025 reform.

## Item pattern

**Labels** — place-first, **no per-NA-term qualifier** (the unit spans multiple
terms). Disambiguate only if a same-name unit with different composition exists
in another era.
- en: `{province} electoral unit No. {n}`
- vi: `Đơn vị bầu cử số {n} tỉnh/thành phố {province}`

**Descriptions** state the verified era + composition + seats, e.g. en:
"electoral unit for National Assembly of Vietnam elections (14th–15th terms,
2016–2021); comprised Thái Nguyên city, Đồng Hỷ and Võ Nhai districts; 3 seats;
superseded by the 2025 administrative reform".

**Statements**
| Property | Value | Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| P31 instance of | Q192611 (electoral unit) | fixed |
| P17 country | Q881 (Vietnam) | fixed |
| P131 located in the administrative territorial entity | province item (for its era) | `locality_id` → QID |
| P1342 number of seats | `seat_count` | data |
| P527 has part(s) | one per component area QID, **qualified** | `districts[]` → QIDs |

**Per-part term qualifiers (preferred over a blanket P576).** Qualify each
`P527 has part(s)` with **P2937 (legislative term)** listing the NA term(s) that
component was in the unit — e.g. `has part → Đồng Hỷ; legislative term: 14th NA
(Q24959152), 15th NA (Q107979136)`. This carries per-part continuity on one
persistent item: a part added or dropped in a later term simply gets a different
set of term qualifiers. P2937's scope constraint explicitly allows "as
qualifier". The unit's active span is then implied by the qualifiers (no term →
not active), so **P576 is unnecessary**; drop it.

Do **not** add P571 inception unless a start is verified. Reference composition
to the source(s): 2021 candidate-list PDF (accessible) and/or Resolution
53/NQ-HĐBCQG (2016), retrieved 2026-01-02. Give the item a description.

NA term QIDs: 14th `Q24959152`, 15th `Q107979136`.

## Batch implications

- Not one item per cycle. **Dedupe identical units across cycles** into a single
  persistent item; the 2025 reform begins a fresh generation of unit items.
- Needs district → QID reconciliation for P527, and cross-cycle composition
  comparison (2011/2016/2021…) to set each unit's span and detect changes.
